ABOUT THE PROJECT

A Mega Integrated Township in Basavakalyan

Anubhava Megacity is designed as a self-sustaining mega township spanning over 100 acres in Basavakalyan. Strategically located near key highway junctions, this project redefines modern community living in North Karnataka.

Equipped with DTCP approvals, 50ft main avenues, 40ft cross roads, LED streetlights, overhead water reservoirs, and dedicated zones for schools, shopping centers, and healthcare.
