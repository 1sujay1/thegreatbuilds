ABOUT THE PROJECT

Sustainable Living in Doddaballapur

Green Meadows Phase I is an 18-acre completed eco-conscious residential development in Doddaballapur. Designed for families seeking green living away from city congestion, it features fruit orchards, rainwater harvesting pits, and solar street lighting.

Equipped with ready A-Katha title deeds, wide internal avenues, and 24/7 security.
