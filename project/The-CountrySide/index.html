ABOUT THE PROJECT

Escape to Serene Nature Near Adiyogi

The CountrySide offers a rare opportunity to own a piece of fertile, green farmland in North Bangalore's fastest-appreciating eco-corridor. Located within minutes of the iconic Adiyogi Shiva Statue in Chikkaballapur, this project combines agricultural bounty with luxury retreat amenities.

Each plot comes with full compound wall fencing, fruit plantations, drip irrigation, and access to a central farmhouse clubhouse. Ideal for organic farming enthusiasts, family holiday retreats, and long-term land wealth creation.
